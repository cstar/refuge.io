Community

If you wish to contribute to the project or simply talk to us, post suggestions, feel free to reach us through:

  • IRC: join us on the channel #refuge.io on freenode.
  • Twitter: we try to answer as well on twitter from the @refugio account.
  • Google+: you can also get news and comment on our google+ page.
We are also preparing a document to explain how to contribute and a new mailing list.

Thank you.
